**Hotel Booking Approval Process**

- When an employee submits a hotel booking request, the RP receives an email notification. 

- Log in to the HRMS portal. 

- From the sidebar, go to Hotel  and select Team Hotel Bookings. 

- The list of employees who have requested hotel bookings will be displayed. 

- Locate the employee’s name in the list. 

- Click on the forward arrow (>) to view complete hotel booking details, such as: 

    - Check-in and check-out dates 

    - City / Hotel Location 

    - Purpose of travel/workshop 

    - After reviewing the details: 

**Approve** – confirm the hotel booking request if you have approval rights. 

**Reject** – if the request is invalid, provide a reason in the pop-up box and click Ok.
