The Reporting Person reviews employee appraisals through the following options:

**Submit Appraisal**
Submit Appraisal allows the reporting person to provide their self-assessment and submit it for review.

**Team Appraisal Lists** 
View and manage the appraisal status of all team members, individual appraisals for review or updates.

**Half Yearly Appraisal**
Conduct and record mid-year performance evaluations, update goals, and give feedback for continuous improvement.

**Steps to Review and Submit Team Appraisals**

- Log in to the HRMS portal. 

- From the sidebar menu, select Appraisal and click Team Appraisal List. 

- Click on an employee’s name to open their Appraisal Form. 

- Review the employee’s self-appraisal details, including achievements, goals, and challenges. 

- Provide your feedback in the Reporting Person section. 

- Add comments and recommendations on performance, training needs, or career development. 

- After submission, the appraisal moves to the next stage. 

 **Half Yearly Appraisal**

 - Log in to the HRMS portal. 

- From the sidebar menu, select Appraisal and click Half Yearly Appraisal. 
- Click on the Appraisal button
- Select the employee 
- Select the employee to be appraised and open the appraisal form.
- Evaluate the employee’s performance for the past six months using the provided form.
- Fill in the ratings, comments, and overall assessment sections.
- Once completed, submit or save the appraisal.
- Upon submission, an email notification will be sent to HR, the reviewer, and the employee.
