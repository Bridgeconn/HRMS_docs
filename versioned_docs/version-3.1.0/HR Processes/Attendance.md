The **Attendance Module** allows HR to manage and monitor attendance for themselves, their teams, and staff across the organization. 

**Apply Attendance**

HR can apply their own attendance such as Work From Home, Outstation â€“ Travel, or Workshop. 

Attendance requests show status as Entitled, Approved, Requested, and Balance. 

**Attendance History**

HR can view their personal past attendance records, including approvals and pending requests. 

**Teams Attendance History**

HR can access attendance data of their direct teams, making it easy to track participation and travel. 

**Staff Attendance**

HR can view overall staff attendance history for better monitoring and reporting. 

This page gives HR a complete overview of staff attendance, helps monitor patterns, and ensures quick decision-making.
