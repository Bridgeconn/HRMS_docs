The Appraisal tab allows HR to manage and monitor the entire employee appraisal process. It includes options to:
- Submit appraisals, 
- View staff appraisal lists 
- Review half-yearly evaluations. 

The page outlines each step of the appraisal workflow, from goal setting and self-assessment to managerial review, HR validation, and final approval, ensuring a transparent and structured performance review cycle.

**Submit Appraisal**
This section allows HR to submit their own self-appraisal by evaluating performance, achievements, and areas of improvement for the appraisal period.

For detailed steps on submitting an appraisal, [Click here](../HRMS%20Features/Performance.md)

**View Staff Appraisal Lists**

The **Staff Appraisal Lists** page displays a list of all employees who have submitted their appraisal forms for the selected cycle. HR can view important details such as Employee ID, Applied Date, Name, Department, Personal Characteristics Score, Work Performance Score, Status, and Verification Status.

The **forward arrow (›)** icon allows HR to view the detailed appraisal form of the selected employee.

The **download icon** enables HR to export or download the appraisal details for record-keeping.

Additionally, the search bar helps to quickly locate an employee by name, and the refresh button reloads the latest appraisal list.

**Staff Half Yearly Appraisal List** 

The Staff Half Yearly Appraisal List page displays a summary of all employee appraisals completed during the half-yearly review cycle. HR can view details such as the Reviewed Date, Employee ID, Name, Department, Reviewer, Overall Performance, and Review Status.

Clicking the forward-pointing arrow (›) beside an employee’s record opens the detailed appraisal form for that employee, allowing HR to review individual ratings, feedback, and comments.

The Print All button enables HR to print all appraisal reports for documentation or reporting purposes.
