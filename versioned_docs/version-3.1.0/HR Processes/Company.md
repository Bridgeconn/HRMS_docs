This section allows HR to manage and share company wide updates with employees. 

**Available Options** 

1. **Announcements** 
HR can post important updates, events, and notices. Notifications are automatically sent to all employees, and they can view both current and past announcements. 

**Steps to post an Announcement**

- HR can create a new announcement by clicking the “+ New” button. 

- A pop-up opens where HR can type the announcement details. 

- Once ready, HR must click “Post Announcement” to publish it. 

Posted announcements are immediately visible to employees, and notifications are sent automatically. 

From this same page, HR can also navigate to the Achievements and Policies tabs to post or update information. 

2. **Achievements** 
HR can highlight company or employee accomplishments. 
These posts notify all employees and help keep everyone updated on progress and milestones. 

**Steps to post an Achievement**

- HR can add new achievements by clicking the “+ New” button. 

- A pop-up opens where HR can type the achievement details (e.g., project success, employee recognition, company milestone). 

- After entering the details, HR must click “Post Achievement” to publish it. 

- Posted achievements are visible to all employees, and notifications are automatically sent. 

From this page, HR can also switch to the Announcements and Policies tabs to manage other updates. 

3. **Policies** 
 HR can upload and categorize company rules, procedures, and guidelines (e.g., leave policy, travel policy, code of conduct). 

**Adding a New Policy** 

When HR clicks on “+ New” under the Policies tab, the Company Policy form opens. 

**HR needs to fill in the following fields**

- **Document No** - Unique policy identifier (auto-following a set format, e.g., BCS-HRD-HRM-001-Rev 0). 

- **Revision** → Policy revision number. 

- **Year** - Year of issue (e.g., 2025). 

- **Description** - Short description of the policy. 

- **Policy Name**- Name of the policy (e.g., Medical Policy, Code of Conduct). 

- **Prepared By** - Name of the HR/preparer. 

- **Checked By** - Reviewer name/department (e.g., MGT). 

- **Approved By** - Approver’s name (e.g., Cdr. Thomas Mathew). 

- **Policy PDF** - Upload option to attach the policy document. 

Once all details are entered, HR clicks “Add Policy” to publish it.The new policy will then appear in the Policies list table, where it can be: 

- Viewed (open and read the file) 

- Deleted (if outdated or incorrect) 

- From this screen, HR can also navigate back to Announcements and Achievements tabs. 

- Employees can view and download policies directly from this section.
